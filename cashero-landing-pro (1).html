Privacy & Security

Your data never leaves your device

No server. No database. No account. Everything lives in your browser — and you can lock it behind a PIN.

💾
Local-only storage
All expenses, budgets, and goals live in your browser's localStorage. No backend receives your financial data — ever. Clearing your browser data is the only way to delete it.
🔐
PIN lock with recovery phrase
Enable a PIN to lock the app on your device. Your data is protected with a hashed key. If you forget the PIN, a recovery phrase lets you back in without losing anything.
📤
Export and own your data
Export everything to CSV any time. No vendor lock-in, no subscription required to get your own history out. It's your data — take it wherever you want.

FAQ

Questions you probably have

Do I need to create an account?
No. Cashero runs entirely in your browser with no account, no email, and no signup. Open the app and start immediately. Your data is saved locally on your device.
Is my financial data safe?
Yes. Everything is stored in your browser's localStorage — no server ever receives your expenses, budgets, or payouts. You can also enable a PIN lock with a recovery phrase for extra protection.
Does it work without internet?
Yes. Cashero is a Progressive Web App (PWA) and caches itself for offline use. You can log expenses, view your dashboard, and check budgets without a connection. AI analysis requires internet.
What is a "payout" in the app?
Your payout is your monthly income — a client payment, a salary transfer, or any money that came in. You enter it manually at the start of each month. Cashero uses it to calculate your savings rate, spending percentages, and health score.
What if I use it on multiple devices?
Since data is stored locally, each device has its own separate data. You can export a CSV from one device and use it as a reference on another. Cross-device sync is not currently available.
Is Cashero really free?
Yes, completely. There is no paid plan, no trial period, and no hidden upgrade wall. The AI analysis features are included at no cost.
